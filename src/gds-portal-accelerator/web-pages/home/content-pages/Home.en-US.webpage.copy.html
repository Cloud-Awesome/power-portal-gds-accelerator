Before you start

To use the service you’ll need:

  • your letter of appointment
  • information on family members going to post
  • information on your dependent children

If you are going overseas and would like to use this service to apply for diplomatic passports you will also need your passport details.

Using the service

This service will take approximately 25 - 45 minutes depending on the size of your family.

If you are unable to complete a section, any information you have already entered will be saved and you can return and complete it at another time.

If you don’t enter any information for more than 20 minutes, you will be signed out.
